Instructions

Please enjoy the augmented reality game that we've developed using the Metaverse platform, especially made for this conference!

The objective of the game is simple: solve three puzzles and unlock a reward. Good luck!

1. Download: Get the Metaverse app (see links below)

2. Scan or Search: In Metaverse, use the QR code on our lobby banner or search 'Adapting New Technology'

3. Play: Solve the three puzzles to unlock a reward!*


*Please note that only phones that support ARCore or ARKit technologies are compatible with this game. If you are unsure, just launch and play the game; it contains an in-game compatibility checker. If your phone is incompatible, you will not be able to complete the game (unless you use a little bit of magic).
